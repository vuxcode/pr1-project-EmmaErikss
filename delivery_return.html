Delivery & Return policy

At Nord Haven, we aim to deliver your order quickly, safely, and with the care your products deserve.

All orders are processed within 1-3 business days, and you will receive a confirmation email once your items have been shipped. Delivery times may vary depending on your location, but typically range from 3-7 business days.

We want you to love your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, you may return most items within 30 days of delivery.

Products must be your return, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Please note that custom-made or personalized items are non-returnable, unless damaged or defective upon arrival.

For more information or assistance with your order, feel free to contact our support team - we're always happy to help.
